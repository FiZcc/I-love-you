# A Love Letter for Belen 💕

This is a heartfelt love letter website created to express genuine remorse, growth, and the desire to rebuild a relationship that meant everything.

## About

Sometimes words alone aren't enough to show someone that you've changed. This site is my attempt to:

- 💭 Acknowledge my mistakes without excuses
- 🌱 Show that I'm genuinely working on becoming better
- 💚 Express how much she means to me
- 🤝 Ask for the chance to prove myself through my actions

## The Message

Belen,

You loved me completely, and I took that for granted. I was careless with your heart, and I'm sorry.

I've changed. Not because I want to win you back (though I do), but because you deserve better, and I deserve to be better.

I'm not asking you to forget. I'm asking you to see who I'm becoming.

If you'll let me, I want to be everything to you again—but better this time.

## How to Use

Just open `index.html` in your browser. It's a beautiful, interactive letter that's meant to be read with an open heart.
