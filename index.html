To Belen

A heartfelt message from someone who's changed

Belen,

I know I haven't been the person you deserved. I know I made mistakes that hurt you, that broke your trust, that made you question everything we had. I can't take those moments back, and I wish I could.

But I want you to know that I've spent time thinking about who I was, about how I treated you, and about how much you meant to me—how much you still mean to me.

You loved me so completely, so genuinely. You saw the best in me when I couldn't see it in myself. And I took that for granted. I was careless with your heart, and there's no excuse for that.

I'm not asking you to forget what happened. I'm asking you to see who I'm becoming. I'm working on myself every single day—not to win you back, but because you deserve better, and I deserve to be better.

The version of me that hurt you is someone I'm leaving behind. The person writing this now understands what you meant to me. The small moments we shared. The way you laughed. The way you believed in me. The way you loved me unconditionally.

I know it's hard to believe in change. I know words are easy. But I'm here, trying every day to show you through my actions, through my growth, through the person I'm becoming.

If you'll let me, I want to be everything to you again—but better this time. More thoughtful. More present. More worthy of the love you gave me.

I'm sorry, Belen. I'm sorry it took me this long to realize what I had.

With all my heart,

Someone who's finally waking up
